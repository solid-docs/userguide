# Log In / Log Out

## Log In

To access your data, and data to which you have permission to access, you must log in:

1. Click the **Log in** button.
2. The **Select your Identity Provider** dialog is displayed:

   ![Select your Identity Provider dialog](/img/Select_Your_Identity_Provider.png)

3. Either enter your WebID into the textbox and click **Go**, or pick your identity provider from the list.
4. The **Login** dialog is presented prompting you to enter your Username and Password:

   ![Node Solid Server Login dialog](/img/Login_Dialog.png)

   :::note
   The actual form of the Login dialog will vary with the version of Solid server in use.
   :::

5. After a successful login, you can view all of the data to which you have read permission within the Pod.

## Log Out

To log out of the Solid account:

1. Open the dropdown menu in the top-right corner of the SolidOS by clicking your Profile image.
2. Select **Log out**.
